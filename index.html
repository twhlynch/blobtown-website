Create the World Around You, One Blob at a Time! Blobtown is a playground for you to create and imagine anything together with your friends.

Create Your Avatar

In Blobtown, it's super easy to create your own avatar! Want to recreate your favorite character? Come up with an OC? Or maybe spend a day as a chair or a bowl of soup? Your imagination is the only limit!

Player Store

Use Blobium, our in-game currency, to buy creations from other players. Whether it's a complete avatar or just some accessories to enhance your own designs, the choice is yours! Think you've made something amazing? Become a seller and start building your own empire!

An ever growing community

Our in-game camera lets you capture your creations to share with the world. There are already tons of Blobtown creators with their own studios and characters. It's super easy to get started!
